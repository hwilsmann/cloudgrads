We’re dedicated to supporting the next generation of grads

We develop the leaders of tomorrow with a blend of technical certification, hands-on professional mentoring and real-world experience over 12 months.

With ongoing mentorship and a series of modules and assessments, our grads are trained not only in modern cloud and data platform technologies but also in the methods of working in the cloud and as part of an Agile delivery team.
We recruit the best talent through a stringent process that recognises technically capable high achievers and team players. We know that diversity in a talented workforce fosters success and we collaborate with Microsoft and top UK universities to ensure it.

Our programme approach

Cloudgrads receive high quality, multi-disciplinary coaching and mentoring to become future digital transformation leaders. As a graduate, you are encouraged to bring tasks, goals, and solutions to the coaching and mentoring sessions. You will also join more than 12,000 people for community-based learning and innovation through meet up groups.
